Pearson Correlation Coefficient (2019-2024): -0.469

A negative correlation score indicates that the stock price and volume are inversely proportional. As stock price goes up, volume goes down, and vice versa. As the correlation score is halfway between 0 (no correlation) and -1 (perfect negative correlation), this relationship is not significant, but not null either. Let's break it down by year.
